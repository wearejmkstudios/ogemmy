Who is OG Emmy

Culture. Confidence. Ambition.

Born and raised in Rivers State, Nigeria, OG Emmy’s sound is a vibrant mix of rhythm and emotion — designed to uplift, connect, and inspire people to dance away their sorrows. His debut single ‘KitKat’, featuring Ghanaian producer Magnom, marked him as a rising force in Afrobeats.

Now based in London, he continues to push the culture forward — a proud cultural ambassador dedicated to uplifting the youth and spreading the sound of Afrobeat worldwide.
